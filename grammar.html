NOUNS

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or idea.

KIND OF NOUN

Noun are classified as follow:

Proper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea or thing

Common: It is the name possessed by things in general.

Collective: It is the name represent a group of thing.

Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotios, condition or action.

PRONOUNS

A pronouns is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun

Kinds of pronoun

Pronouns are classified as follow:

Personal pronoun: It refers to a specific noun and used as substitute of the noun

Interrogative pronoun: It stand as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing

Indefinite pronoun: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentences and at the same time connects two statement.

Reflexive pronoun: It usually follow a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
